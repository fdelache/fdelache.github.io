Being Lazy

Being Lazy is a state of mind. How can we make sure computers work for us and not the reverse? How can we reduce the amount of effort required to use software, maintain it and reason about it?

Not that it's easy, but one can try to make it easier.

Background

I started Software development in 2000. Since then, I always try to simplify things where possible, and having fun while doing so. Currently working at Shopify as a Staff developer. You can find me on Bluesky.
