The complete content of this page is written by me. The images used are either photographs I took myself, or art made be me. No Generative AI was used for these parts.

I do use AI assistants to help me write the software underlying this blog site, as well as proofread my writing (as a non English native speaker). But AI is only a tool used to understand and write code, not for expressing my own thoughts. A little bit of authenticity is aways welcome, and this is what I'm striving for.

Why this page you might ask? I stumbled on this Cassidy's bluesky post, which emphasize the /ai manifesto. Found that it was a good idea :-)
